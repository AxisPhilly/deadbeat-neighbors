Tax-delinquent property impact

There are approximately 90,000 tax delinquent properties in Philadelphia. These properties reduce the overall value of other properties, even if those properties are not tax delinquent. This is called the "diminished value".

Hover over the map to see the effect of tax delinquent properties on the neighborhood level. Search for your property in the box below to see the impact of tax delinquent properties on your own property. Note: If your property is not found, it was modeled--but it may still be impacted.
